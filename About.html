About Cloud 7

Supporting mental wellness through compassion, understanding, and safe spaces

Our Mission

Supporting Those Who Struggle with Mental Blocks

Cloud 7 was created to address a fundamental truth: mental health challenges are real, valid, and deserve compassionate support. We've experienced firsthand how difficult it can be to express feelings and seek help.

That's why we've built a platform that prioritizes listening, understanding, and providing safe spaces for healing—whether through mood tracking, journaling, or connecting with professionals who care.

The Problem We Solve

  • Difficulty expressing feelings and emotions
  • Fear of judgment when seeking help
  • Mental blocks preventing goal achievement
  • Limited access to affordable mental health support

Our Core Values

  • Compassion First: We believe in listening before solving, understanding before advising.
  • Privacy & Trust: Your journey is yours alone. We protect your data and confidentiality above all.
  • Community Support: No one should face mental health challenges alone. We're building a supportive community.
  • Innovation: Combining technology with human care to make mental health support accessible.

What Makes Us Different

Listening Over Solutions

We understand that sometimes people need to be heard more than they need advice. Our platform emphasizes journaling and reflection, giving you space to process emotions at your own pace.

Anonymity & Safety

For those who aren't comfortable sharing their identity, we offer anonymous session options. Creating a sense of safety is our top priority.

Affordable Access

Mental health support shouldn't be a luxury. We offer affordable subscription models and partnerships with NHS, schools, and employers to make help accessible.

Track Your Progress

Mood tracking and journaling create a tangible record of your journey. Looking back at how far you've come can be incredibly motivating.

Our Partners

Working Together for Better Mental Health

  • NHS: Partnered for accessible mental health services
  • Schools & Universities: Supporting student mental wellness programs
  • Employers: Workplace mental health initiatives
  • Licensed Professionals: Network of qualified therapists and counselors

A Personal Journey

"As someone who's personally struggled with mental health, and has known others who have also struggled, I know how important it is to have a listening ear rather than someone who strictly gives solutions to problems."

Cloud 7 was built from this understanding. It's more than a platform—it's a commitment to supporting every person on their unique mental wellness journey, providing the tools and connections they need to heal and grow.
